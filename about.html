About Us

Team Bernie LA is a collaborative effort between various self-organized groups and individual volunteers in the Greater Los Angeles area of California. Our aim is to build and connect local grassroots organizations for Bernie in order to maintain a positive presence in every neighborhood at all times. If you'd like to volunteer, list your team or event, or just want to connect, please get in touch at contact@teamberniela.org! This site is run purely by volunteers and is not affiliated with the official Bernie Sanders campaign.
